about me

agile mentality

As organizations shift a bulk of their team work to the Cloud, iterative and self-organized individuals become critical components of success. For this reason, I am deeply committed to Agile and its 12 Principles of software development.

quality and testing

In order to ensure an exceptional User Experience (UX) and an effective User Interface (UI), I am accustomed to dedicating many hours to the meticulous testing of the front interface and validating it against the back-end data.

documentation

Keeping records of my work is second-nature to me. Accurate and up-to-date project documentation enhances face-to-face communication and establishes a shared history for future generations of employees to look to for knowledge and inspiration.
